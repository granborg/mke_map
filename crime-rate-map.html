How the tiers are measured

Tiers reflect reported Part I offenses (violent crimes such as homicide, robbery, and aggravated assault, plus property crimes such as burglary, theft, and vehicle theft) per 1,000 residents per year, expressed relative to the Milwaukee citywide average. Figures are approximate, based on Milwaukee Police Department reporting patterns through early 2026.
