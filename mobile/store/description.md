## Description (max 4,000 chars)

MKE Neighborhood Watch puts Milwaukee's 190 neighborhoods on a live map,
color-coded into five crime-rate tiers, and tells you which neighborhood
you're standing in right now.

WHAT IT DOES

- Full-city map with every neighborhood shaded by its crime-rate tier,
  from Lowest to Highest
- Live banner showing the neighborhood you're currently in and its tier
- An alert when you cross into a High or Highest tier neighborhood, so
  you're never surprised by where you've ended up
- Works entirely on-device: the neighborhood boundaries are bundled with
  the app, and your location never leaves your phone

HOW THE TIERS WORK

Tiers are indicative bands of reported Part I offenses per 1,000
residents per year, anchored to Milwaukee's citywide average — not exact
published per-neighborhood figures. They are a rough orientation aid,
not a precise safety score. Crime statistics reflect reported incidents
only and can't capture everything about a neighborhood; many factors
they don't measure make a place a good or bad spot to be.

PRIVACY

Your location is used only on your device to determine the current
neighborhood. Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.
